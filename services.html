IDSPL has been working in the building energy efficiency sector for the past 4 years. The specialities of IDSPL include policy advocacy, development and implementation for Central, State, Local and International agencies, Building Energy Performance analysis for ECBC compliance, Green building rating systems and Building Energy optimization, Lighting design for buildings, HVAC System design, peer review and monitoring systems. Renewable Energy Integration

Renewable Energy Integration addresses environmentally responsible, energy efficient, and Sustainable Building Design. Our approach towards Integrating Renewable Energy is associated with ‘demand’ side reduction as a principle through smart and energy efficient design of the building services. The renewable energy study will evaluate energy efficiency, energy demand, carbon savings, capital costs, annual operating costs, payback period and technology comparisons to make an easy decision. Our multi-disciplinary expertise will deliver comprehensive techno-commercial feasibility analysis for renewable energy applications.

Founder

Mr. Kanagaraj Ganesan has an experience of nine years in the field of ‘Building Energy Efficiency’. He is a licensed architect, Certified Measurement and Verification Professional (CMVP), and Energy Conservation Building Code (ECBC) Master Trainer. He is a post-graduate from the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, in the ‘Building Energy Efficiency’ domain.

Mr. Ganesan is the founder of Integrative Design Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (IDSPL). IDSPL focuses on mainstreaming “integrated design” for addressing sustainability in the built environment. He is also working as a consultant to UNDP India and the All India Institute of Local Self Government (AIILSG) for the implementation of ECBC in India.

Until June 2015, he was working as a Senior Programme Officer at Greentech Knowledge Solutions Pvt. Ltd., New Delhi, which has been appointed as the Indian Project Management and Technical Unit (PMTU) for the Indo-Swiss Building Energy Efficiency Project (BEEP).
